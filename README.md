1.)**Solar-powered lighting**

**Solar-powered lighting is a lighting system that uses energy from the sun to produce light. It works by converting sunlight into electricity through a solar panel. The electricity is stored in a rechargeable battery and later used to power LED lights, especially at night or during power outages.**


**Main Components**


**Solar Panel – collects sunlight and converts it into electrical energy.
Rechargeable Battery – stores the energy produced by the solar panel.**

**Charge Controller – regulates charging and protects the battery from overcharging.**

**LED Light – provides efficient illumination using low power.
Switch or Sensor – controls the light manually or automatically.**


**How It Works**


**During the daytime, the solar panel absorbs sunlight and charges the battery. At night, the stored energy powers the LED light automatically or through a switch.**

**Advantages**


**Saves electricity costs
Uses renewable energy
Environment friendly
Easy to install in remote areas
Useful during brownouts or emergencies
Requires little maintenance**


**Common Uses**

**Street lights
Garden and pathway lights
Home emergency lighting
Security lights**


**Camping and outdoor lighting**


**Solar-powered lighting is a practical and sustainable solution for providing illumination without relying heavily on the electrical grid.**


2.) **Temperature Level Indicator**


**A temperature level indicator is a device or system used to measure and display the temperature of a substance, environment, or equipment. It helps monitor whether the temperature is low, normal, or high, and is commonly used in homes, industries, laboratories, vehicles, and electronic systems.**


**Main Components**

**Temperature Sensor – Detects temperature changes (e.g., thermistor, thermostat, or temperature probe).**

**Control Circuit – Processes the sensor signal.**

**Display/Indicator – Shows the temperature level using LEDs, digital displays, gauges, or alarms.**

**Power Supply – Provides electrical energy to operate the device.**

**How It Works**

**The sensor senses the surrounding temperature.**


**The signal is sent to the control circuit**

**The indicator displays the temperature reading or level.**

**Some systems activate alarms or cooling devices if the temperature exceeds a set limit.**


**Types of Temperature Indicators**

**Analog indicators – Use needles or color scales.**

**Digital indicators – Display exact temperature values on screens.**

**LED indicators – Use colored lights to show temperature ranges.**

**Smart temperature indicators – Connected to computers or mobile apps for monitoring.**

**Applications**

**Weather monitoring**

**Refrigerators and air conditioners**

**Industrial machines**

**Medical equipment**

**Automobiles**

**Food storage systems**

**Advantages**

**Helps prevent overheating or freezing**

**Improves safety and equipment protection**

**Provides accurate temperature monitoring**

**Easy to use and maintain**

**A temperature level indicator is important for maintaining proper operating conditions and ensuring safety in many environments.**




3.)**Polvoron Vending Machine**


**A polvoron vending machine is an automated dispensing machine designed to sell polvoron, a popular powdered milk candy and shortbread delicacy commonly found in the Philippines. The machine allows customers to purchase polvoron quickly and conveniently without the need for a cashier or store attendant.**

**Main Components**

**Product Storage Compartment – Holds individually packed polvoron products.**

**Dispensing Mechanism – Releases the selected item after payment.**

**Payment System – Accepts coins, bills, cards, or digital payments.**

**Control System – Processes customer selections and controls machine operations.**

**Display Panel – Shows product choices, prices, and instructions.**

**Power Supply – Provides electricity to operate the machine.**

**How It Works**

**Customers select their preferred polvoron flavor or package.**

**Payment is inserted or processed electronically.**

**The machine verifies the payment.**

**The dispensing system releases the selected polvoron item.**

**Change is returned if necessary.**

**Features**

**Automatic product dispensing**

**Multiple flavor selections**

**Cashless payment capability**

**User-friendly interface**

**Secure product storage**

**Advantages**

**Convenient and fast service**

**Operates with minimal manpower**

**Available 24/7**

**Reduces long customer queues**

**Helps promote local delicacies**
