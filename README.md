1.)**Solar-powered lighting**

**Solar-powered lighting is a lighting system that uses energy from the sun to produce light. It works by converting sunlight into electricity through a solar panel. The electricity is stored in a rechargeable battery and later used to power LED lights, especially at night or during power outages.**


**Main Components**


**'Solar Panel – collects sunlight and converts it into electrical energy.
Rechargeable Battery – stores the energy produced by the solar panel.
Charge Controller – regulates charging and protects the battery from overcharging.'**

**'LED Light – provides efficient illumination using low power.
Switch or Sensor – controls the light manually or automatically.'**

**How It Works**


**During the daytime, the solar panel absorbs sunlight and charges the battery. At night, the stored energy powers the LED light automatically or through a switch.**

**Advantages**


**Saves electricity costs
Uses renewable energy
Environment friendly
Easy to install in remote areas
Useful during brownouts or emergencies
Requires little maintenance**


**Common Uses**

**Street lights
Garden and pathway lights
Home emergency lighting
Security lights**


**Camping and outdoor lighting**


**Solar-powered lighting is a practical and sustainable solution for providing illumination without relying heavily on the electrical grid.**
